K-DRAMA

Profile

A Korean drama (Korean: 한국 드라마; RR: Han-guk deurama), more popularly known as Koreanovela or K-drama, is a type of television series in the Korean language made in South Korea. They are popular worldwide, especially in Asia, partially due to the spread of Korean popular culture (the "Korean Wave") and their widespread availability via streaming services, which often offer subtitles in multiple languages.
